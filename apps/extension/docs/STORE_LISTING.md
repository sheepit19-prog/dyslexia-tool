**Detailed Description** (HTML allowed):
```html
<p>Dyslexia Tool is a privacy-first Chrome extension designed to support dyslexic users while reading and writing online. Our intelligent companion offers discreet assistance exactly when you need it—helping you communicate with confidence.</p>

<h2>Key Features</h2>

<h3>📖 Dyslexia-Friendly Font</h3>
<p>Apply easy-to-read fonts to any webpage with one click. Reduces visual stress and improves reading comfort.</p>

<h3>🔊 Text-to-Speech</h3>
<p>Listen to any selected text read aloud. Perfect for proofreading your writing or consuming long articles.</p>

<h3>📏 Reading Ruler</h3>
<p>A visual guide that highlights the current line you're reading. Helps maintain focus and reduces line-skipping.</p>

<h3>✨ Smart Spelling Suggestions</h3>
<p>Our companion detects when you're struggling with spelling and offers helpful suggestions. Only activates when you need it—never intrusive.</p>

<h3>🎤 Voice Notes</h3>
<p>Capture quick audio memos when typing is challenging. Store up to 50 notes per month locally on your device.</p>

<h3>🤝 Companion Mode</h3>
<p>Intelligent assistance that learns your writing patterns. Offers help after detecting struggle (multiple backspaces or long pauses)—always dismissible.</p>

<h2>Privacy First</h2>
<p>Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All features work locally in your browser.</p>
<ul>
  <li>✅ No account required</li>
  <li>✅ No data sent to servers</li>
  <li>✅ All voice notes stored locally</li>
  <li>✅ No browsing history tracking</li>
  <li>✅ Open source transparency</li>
</ul>

<h2>How It Works</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Install the extension</li>
  <li>Click the Dyslexia Tool icon in your toolbar</li>
  <li>Enable features as needed (font, reading ruler, companion mode)</li>
  <li>Start typing or reading—help appears when you need it</li>
</ol>

<h2>Perfect For</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Students writing essays and emails</li>
  <li>Professionals drafting documents</li>
  <li>Anyone with dyslexia, dysgraphia, or reading difficulties</li>
  <li>ESL learners working on spelling</li>
</ul>

<h2>Permissions Explained</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>activeTab</strong> - Apply features to the current webpage</li>
  <li><strong>storage</strong> - Save your settings and voice notes locally</li>
  <li><strong>offscreen</strong> - Record voice notes (required by Chrome)</li>
  <li><strong>&lt;all_urls&gt;</strong> - Work on any website where you need assistance</li>
</ul>

<h2>Support</h2>
<p>Have questions or feedback? We'd love to hear from you!</p>
<p><strong>Email:</strong> [your-email@example.com]</p>
<p><strong>Privacy Policy:</strong> [your-privacy-policy-url]</p>

<h2>Built with Care</h2>
<p>Dyslexia Tool is developed with input from the dyslexic community. We believe assistive technology should be empowering, not stigmatizing.</p>

<p><em>Version 0.1.0 - MVP Release</em></p>
```
